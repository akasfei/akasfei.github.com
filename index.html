    • INTRODUCTORY SEQUENCE

      What is SFEI Systems ISN?

      I-S-N stands for Imaging Sequential Network. It is associated with SFEI Systems, which is named after its author, SFEI. It is a website which stores SFEI's hightlighted projects as well as a brief introduction of himself.

      ISN features a sequantial layout, including sequence groups, sequences, and sequence nodes. Sequences with different catagories are arranged in different sequence groups and each sequence represents a specific topic. A sequence consists of several nodes, which represents articles explaining the corresponding topic.

      You can use arrow keys, enter and backspace to navigate through the nodes and sequences.

    • INTRODUCTORY SEQUENCE

      OPERATING REFERENCE

      You can use arrow keys, enter and backspace to navigate through the nodes and sequences. Specific key navigations are:

      When a sequence node is active, use and keys to navigate through other nodes. Use and keys to move to the next or previous sequence. Use ENTER to active the default button (the blue one), and BACKSPACE to move up to the parent sequence.

      When a sequence is active, use and keys to navigate through other sequences. Use and keys to activate the last or first node in this sequence. Use ENTER to active the first node, and BACKSPACE to move up to the parent sequence group.

      When a sequence group is active, use and keys to navigate through other groups. Use ENTER to active the first sequence in this group.

    • WE-BS

      WHAT'S THIS

      In general, this is a page assembler using Grunt and assemble. This poject works as a templete for static websites, and it comes with the popular front-end framework Bootstrap. You can create many pages in hbs format, and then assemble into fully function webpages.

      There are many tools you can use to simplify the development process, like including partials, or iterating arrays to create many lines of code of the same format. Other tools like LESS, UGLIFY and JSHINT are also supported to help lint and organize CSS and JavaScript code.

      VIEW ON GITHUB
    • WE-BS

      WHY I BUILD IT

      I created this project to optimize and enhance my typical static webpage design & development procedure. First problem this project solved was the layout problem - I have no layout files before, just pages. If I wanted to make a slight change to the layout, I had to modify all webpages.

      By applying Grunt, I can also use LESS more easily by grunt-contrib-less which will automaticly help me compile css files from less. Besides LESS, I also applied other tools, like jshint and uglify. So with minimum configuration, this project can help developers create better and faster websites.

      Officially released on Mar 30, 2013, Springfield project (aka "Spring Festival Soiree Discovery") features a journey-like experience that will entertain as well as educate visitors about China's Spring Festival Soiree, aka "Chun wan".     • ASSETBASE

      SUMMARY

      The assetbase is created to be a host for several js libries including jQuery, bootstrap, and backbone.js.

      This website provides three popular JavaScript libaries. It is designed because I cannot tolerate the latency of ajax.google.com in mainland China (the reason is quite clear). This website supports both http and https.

      Generally speaking, when users request a file without other requests (for example /jQuery/), the server reads its cache and send the file back. It is also possible to request a file of specific version, and whether it should be compressed.

    • A World without Holidays

      SUMMARY

      The A World without Holidays trilogy is about a persistant struggle of a group of students (the NOVICE) against the evil faction (the MENTORS), who intend to use their power and influence to cancel all holidays and achieve their evil goal of nation-wide domination. The story focuses on two of the students, who discovered, investigated and finally sabotaged the MENTORS' plot.

      The first part of the trilogy was shot in 2009, while the second and third part are rendered using World of Warcraft graphics engine in 2011 and 2012. Due to some complicated reasons, only the third part is publicly released.

    • A World without Holidays

      STORY

      The story of A World without Holidays is based the irony on China's current high school education system, in which students have a sole goal to fight for: the Colledge Entrance Examination. In order to achieve better score, students are usually required to study longer and take more classes. The story of the trilogy starts with a direct conflict between the two faction, both are trying to obtain an artifact to achieve their own goals. The conflict, however, led to the disappearance of the artifact. The two factions had no choice but to negotiate peace.

      Five years later, a third faction named Al'Asad Education appeared and posed great threat to both faction. So the school selected some students and formed Task Force 320, who later discovered that the school wished only to benefit from the conflict. TF320 stopped cooperating with the school, and as a consequence, they were ambushed, captured and later executed. However, a member of TF320 was rescued by a stranger and survived.

      Finally, with the help of some students, two heroes are able to sabotage the organization of the MENTORS. They took down the MENTORS' defences piece by piece, and they finally succeeded in putting the leader of the MENTORS out of rule.

    • A World without Holidays

      EPISODE 1: HARMORIZATION

      In this episode, both the NOVICE and the MENTORS are looking for an artifact named "Weizi Stanp", and both sent their best man to capture the artifact. Our hero, Dongqi Wang, was sent by the NOVICE.

      In order to enter the city where the artifact is guarded, Wang had to enter the Star of Despair Contest of English. After winning the first few contests, he was invited to the finals. Before the final, he managed to locate, secure and extract the artifact. However, on his way back, he encountered a group of city managers and was captured and sent to Grandpa Zhai's Removal Center of Escaping Class Addiction.

      The artifact, however, disappeared. So the MENTORS and the NOVICE had to negociate peace.

    • A World without Holdiays

      EPISODE 2: PASSING

      Five years later, A third faction appeared. Named Al'Asad Education, they claimed that their classes can improve the students' exam score. The MENTORS selected three students and formed Task Force 320, and sent them to investigate the third faction. During an undercover mission, a member of the Task Force was captured.

      As the investigation goes on, the Task Force found an unusual connection between the third faction and the MENTORS. In the same time, Al'Asad Education managed to turn a radical group of students against the school. During a raid to a base of Al'Asad Education, TF320 rescued their captured member and tried to cease the destructive uprising.

      Fearing that TF320 might have discovered their plot, the MENTORS ambushed TF320. All but one menber were captured and executed. The last member of TF320, named Shrink, was rescued by a stranger, who later turned out to be Dongqi Wang, the hero from episode 1.

    • A World without Holidays

      EPISODE 3: DISTANT LANDSs

      A month after episode 2, the radical group launched an attack on the school's facilites. The uprising was supressed by brute force, and using it as an excuse, the MENTORS announced that all holidays will be cancelled. In the same time, Dongqi Wang and Shrink came up with a plan that will take down the MENTORS' insitituion step by step.

      With the help of some menbers of the NOVICE, they managed to sabotage the MENTORS' main military force and financial income. However, in order to confront the leader of the MENTORS, Wang had to deliberately walk into a trap. Wang sacrificed himself for a chance to strike.

      After officially joining the NOVICE, Shrink infaltrated the office of the MENTORS, where he defeated the leader of the MENTORS. The leader of the NOVICE, in the same time, assassinated the leader of Al'Asad Education. Finally, using the power of Weizi Stamp, they were able to restore the school to its previous glory.

    • ABOUT ISN

      DESIGN CONCEPT

      The design of this webpage is inspired by the DNA Tracker of the Assassin's Creed Series. Like the DNA tracker, this webpage has mulitple sequences available to expand and read the information in each node.

      With the increase in the amount of the seuqences, some sequence will eventually move out of the screen. That's why when moving to the sequence on the right, the sequence container will move left a bit, thus making the active sequence located around the middle of the screen.

      Currently this webpage is static. But after the development of my API Server (possibly SFEI Systems v3.5.x) I will add a js plugin that will fetch new projects from my server.
